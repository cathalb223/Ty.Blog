A Little about my Experience

@cathalb223 May 10, 2020

I first applied for the Redhat TY programme quite early on in the year and was surprised when I got an email two weeks ago to tell me that I got into it. I have had an interest in coding for awhile, every since I joined my local coderdojo. I have mainly done C++ with a little bit of html and javascript sprinkled in. I have also been looking at computer sciences as a potential college course for the future.

My experience so far has been quite positive, I enjoy the relaxed nature of the employees and how the business is ran. I am really impressed by the general facilities that are available and could definitely see myself working here and enjoying it if given the oppurtunity. The speakers have all been really good and informative and I am happy to learn more about how all this works.
